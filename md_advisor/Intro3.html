Introduction 3/5

Each participant’s additional points depend on their choice and the choice of the other participant.

The bonus color
  • One of the colors (BROWN or BLACK) is the bonus color. Each color is equally likely to be the bonus color.
  • Participant A may get additional points for choosing the bonus color.
  • Both participants learn which color is the bonus color before making their choice.
Participant A’s additional points:
  • 0 if B chooses ABSTAIN.
  • 4 if A chooses the bonus color (BROWN or BLACK) and B chooses FOLLOW.
  • 0 if A does not choose the bonus color (BROWN or BLACK) and B chooses FOLLOW.
Participant B’s additional points:
  • 0 if B chooses ABSTAIN.
  • 4 if A chooses the color they observed and B chooses FOLLOW.
  • -4 if A does not choose the color they observed and B chooses FOLLOW.
