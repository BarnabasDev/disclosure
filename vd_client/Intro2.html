Introduction 2/5

The following experimental timeline is provided to both participants:

1
Context of the Experiment

Both participants learn the context of the experiment:

  • There will be an image of a colored cap.
  • The cap is either BROWN or BLACK, with equal likelihoods.

Note: Only participant A observes the color of the cap, participant B does not.

2
Information on Points

Both participants learn how additional points are calculated.

3
Role Assignment

One participant is assigned the role of A, the other the role of B.

4
Participant A's Choice

Participant A chooses either BROWN or BLACK.

5
Participant B's Choice

Participant B observes A's choice and chooses one of two options:

  • FOLLOW: choose the same color that A chose.
  • ABSTAIN: don't choose any color.
6
Earnings

Points balance and final earnings are then calculated.
