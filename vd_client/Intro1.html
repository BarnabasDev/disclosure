Introduction 1/5

Participants and roles
  • In this experiment, there are two participants, participant A and participant B.
  • You are matched with another real participant and assigned either the role of A or B.
  • Both you and the other participant get the same instructions.
Points and earnings
  • Both you and the other participant get an endowment of 10 points each.
  • You can get additional points, depending on the choices made in the experiment.
    Details on additional points will be provided later.
  • Your final earnings are the sum of your participation fee and bonus payment.
  • Your bonus payment is determined by your points balance:
Points balance = {{ C.FIXED_ADVISOR_WAGE }} points endowment + additional points
Bonus payment = Points balance 20 × $1
Final earnings = participation fee + bonus payment
