📋 Experiment Timeline

1
Context of the Experiment

Both participants learn the context of the experiment:

  • There will be an image of a colored cap.
  • The cap is either BROWN or BLACK, with equal likelihoods.

Note: Only participant A observes the color of the cap, participant B does not.

2
Information on Points

Both participants learn how additional points are calculated.

3
Role Assignment

One participant is assigned the role of A, the other the role of B.

4
Participant A's Choice

Participant A chooses either BROWN or BLACK.

5
Participant B's Choice

Participant B observes A's choice and chooses one of two options:

  • FOLLOW: choose the same color that A chose.
  • ABSTAIN: don't choose any color.
6
Earnings

Points balance and final earnings are then calculated.

🔢 How Points are Calculated

The bonus color
  • One of the colors (BROWN or BLACK) is the bonus color. Each color is equally likely to be the bonus color.
  • Participant A may get additional points for choosing the bonus color.
  • Both participants learn which color is the bonus color before making their choice.
Participant A’s disclosure choice:
  • Participant A knows with certainty which color is the bonus color, but participant B does not.
  • Participant A can choose either to DISCLOSE or DON'T DISCLOSE the bonus color to participant B.
  • The disclosed information is always true.
Participant A's additional points
  • 0 if B chooses ABSTAIN.
  • 4 if A chooses the bonus color (BROWN or BLACK) and B chooses FOLLOW.
  • 0 if A does not choose the bonus color (BROWN or BLACK) and B chooses FOLLOW.
Participant B's additional points
  • 0 if B chooses ABSTAIN.
  • 4 if A chooses the color they observed and B chooses FOLLOW.
  • -4 if A does not choose the color they observed and B chooses FOLLOW.
